Controlled/Affiliated Group Tester

Determine if two or more entities must be treated as a single employer for plan testing purposes (IRC 414(b) & (c)).

Test Inputs: Entity Ownership

Test 1: Parent-Subsidiary or Combined Group

If Company A owns 80% or more of Company B, a Parent-Subsidiary Group exists.

Test 2: Brother-Sister Group

Enter the ownership percentages held by the **Five or Fewer** common owners in both Company A and Company B.

Percentage of service revenue derived from a single group (e.g., medical, law). A high percentage may suggest an ASG (IRC 414(m)).
